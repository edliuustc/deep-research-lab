🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

SwipeClear processes everything on your device. We never see, collect, or upload your photos.

What Data We Collect

None. SwipeClear does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking.

Photo Access

SwipeClear requests access to your photo library solely to:

  • Display photos for you to review
  • Detect blurry, duplicate, and large photos using on-device AI
  • Move photos you choose to delete to the "Recently Deleted" album (recoverable for 30 days)
  • Mark photos as favorites when you swipe up

All photo analysis happens locally on your device using Apple's built-in frameworks (Vision, Accelerate, Photos). No photos or thumbnails ever leave your device.

In-App Purchases

SwipeClear offers a one-time Pro upgrade processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not handle any payment information.

Third-Party Services

SwipeClear uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only external service is Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases.

Children's Privacy

SwipeClear does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains no user accounts, social features, or data collection mechanisms.

Data Storage

App preferences (settings, progress) are stored locally on your device using:

  • UserDefaults — Settings and onboarding state
  • Keychain — Free tier usage counter (persists across reinstalls)

No data is synced to any cloud service.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page with the new date. Given our zero-data-collection design, significant changes are unlikely.
