SwipeClear is a photo organization app that helps you clean up your photo library by identifying blurry photos, duplicates, screenshots, and large files. All processing occurs locally on your device.

Photo Deletion

When you choose to delete photos through SwipeClear, they are moved to the "Recently Deleted" album in your Photos app. You can recover them within 30 days. After 30 days, Apple permanently removes them. SwipeClear is not responsible for photos deleted after the recovery period.
